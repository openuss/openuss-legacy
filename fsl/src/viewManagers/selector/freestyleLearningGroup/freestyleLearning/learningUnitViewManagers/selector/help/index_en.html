Selector Help

The Selector View offers the possibility of associative learning control for terms, illustrations and symbols of an original or competitive domain, based on the idea of an Edutainment Software. The didactical concept of the Selector View is to represent the technical terms on a playing field with pictures or text elements which are coincidental loaded or exchanged after an adjustable time interval. The Lerner has to click within this time on the symbols associated with an upper topic and receives game points. The illustration represented down gives an overview of the fundamental structure of the Selector View playing field.


After a run is activated over the start button from the Interaction panel, the user is able to do his individual play attitudes, e.g. to specify the playing field size, the play speed, as well as the length of the run in seconds.


At the end of a play run the play results are evaluated.

View specific Element Interaction Buttons:


Starts a selector run.


Stops a selector run.
