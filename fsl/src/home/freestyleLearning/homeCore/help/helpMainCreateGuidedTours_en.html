Craete Guided Tours

Guided Tours can be created in the author and in the learner role. An author produces Guided Tours, he would like to give the studying. The Guided Tours delivered with a Learning Unit can make the getting into the Learning Unit easier, represent a summary of the contents or support at seminar house-works or special questions.

Select "Tour / Save" from the menu to save a tour. In the upcoming dialog you can name the tour and assign a picture. Your new tour can be selected and started by all local free-style's Learning users.
